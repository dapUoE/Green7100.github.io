Chapter 23 - Louis

"Emperor Vincent, may I have a moment?"

Hearing a voice from behind, Vincent Vollachia came to a stop. Even before turning around, he knew who the voice belonged to. He never forgot a voice or a face he'd once encountered. The voice belonged to a peddler with blond hair and blue eyes - Flop O'Connell.

"Unfortunately, we're in the midst of a crisis threatening the Empire's existence. I don't have time to engage in idle chatter."

He knew who it was, but Vincent was not inclined to entertain Flop. He needed to discuss the oracle just received from Ubilk the Star Reader, with Berstetz, Celine, and others about how to handle it. While they had Groovy as the designated countermeasure against the enemy, relying on him was irrational at its peak considering he could be dead by now. In that regard, it would be less absurd to have hopes on Natsuki Subaru, who was headed to persuade the other person named in the prophecy.

To begin with, Vincent didn't see how he could convince the girl. Even a proper communication with her seemed difficult. As long as his play did not work on the girl, it made sense to let Subaru catch one of the two lights. Thus, all Vincent could do was contemplate practical strategies---

"Whoa, whoa, you can't tell if it's idle chatter until you hear it out, can you? You wouldn't know what kind of story I have up my sleeve, even with your wisdom, dear Emperor!"

"You..."

As Vincent tried to resume his walk, Flop reached out, grabbed his shoulder, and held him back. It was an act of recklessness so profound that it could only be called sheer madness. Considering the situation, it was an act tantamount to asking for Flop's head to be chopped off instantly.

"Let go of me right now, or you'll lose your life."

"Of course, I would like to tell you right away too. However, I need some time to properly tell the story. It won't be quick."

"I didn't ask you to speak, I asked you to let go--"

"Come on, don't be like that, Abel-chin. Just go along with what uncle has to say."

"What!?"

As he glared at Flop, hand still on his shoulder, another voice came from in front of him. Suddenly, hands were sliding under his arms and lifting his body, an act of violence that entirely caught him off guard. Holding their breathes, the two speaking were the bright-eyed Medium O'Connell and her sibling, Flop, both their smiles enveloping Vincent from either side.

"Abel, you've been avoiding big-brother all this time, haven’t you? Big-brother is so bruised and battered he can barely stand! Instead of being mean, listen to his story, okay?"

"Don’t talk nonsense. How long will you continue to interact so casually with me? When the situation changes, the status changes too. It’s not like we’re in the Shudrak village or the castle city anymore."

"Well, Abel, you may be playing emperor, but I think it’s wrong to disregard us like that! Am I right, big-brother?"

"Yeah, sis!"

The stubborn siblings didn’t lend an ear to Vincent's words.

At the high-spirited sister's call, a vigorous response from Flop, who then opened the door of the adjacent room. Medium ushered Vincent inside, the door closing behind.

Quickly taking the Emperor hostage, it was there that Medium finally released Vincent.

"Do you realize that this is a brutal act that not only affects you two but could implicate your entire family?"

"Ha, ha, ha, ha! Too bad, dear village head. Our family consists only of us brothers and sisters. So, your threat doesn't scare us!"

"But, what about the kids at the orphanage, big-brother? Even if we're not blood-related, those who fled are still our family!"

"Ha, ha, ha, ha! Once you mention it! Village head, I wonder what we should do to get your forgiveness!"

"---Release me immediately and stay quiet."

Regardless of the circumstances, the O'Connell siblings’ behavior did not change.

It was Medium, with her stretching ability, and allegedly injured Flop, that were now under suspicion.

Normally, if one's body seems to shrink, it would put a huge strain on the mental state, yet Medium seemed too ignorant of its effect.

Flop too, despite being heavily injured, appeared hale - although the blood veins in his neck, and his pale, nearly whitewashed face, were concealed with makeup.

"The appearance of a merchant is often just as important."

"------""Normally, the golden rule of business is to buy and sell what is desired, but I'm afraid I can't meet your current request, village chief. I don't want to put this off any longer."

"What are you talking about..."

"I'm just the messenger. It's a message from the person who was playing the emperor instead of you."

Vincent's eyes slightly widened, anticipating more nonsense. The message that the O'Connell siblings had chosen to convey to Vincent, even going so far as imprisoning the emperor in an empty room—it was only given to one person in this world.

Therefore...

"You should listen to his words, village chief. No, Emperor Vincent Vollachia," Flop said, revealing a serious expression for the first time. Vincent did not choose his words to dispute him.

"The Archbishop of Gluttony, Louis Arneb."

When Subaru acknowledged it aloud, he heard a loud creaking sound inside his chest. It was like a forbidden box that always within reach but he had been hesitant to open. Like Pandora's Box, always able to be opened if he so chose.

"----"

Deciding that the hallway was not the place for their conversation, Subaru and his group rented out a large guestroom. Abel had thoughtfully suggested they use it if necessary—it was a rare consideration of human feelings from him. Of course, it made sense given that this matter could be a matter of great importance for the empire.

In that guestroom, members of the Emilia faction gathered: Emilia, Beatrice, Otto, Garfiel, and Roswaal. Additionally...

"The Archbishop of Gluttony... Frankly, it's a little too common for us to be dealing with."

Anastasia quietly muttered, resting her hand on her cheek. She and Julius, who stood silently by her side, were also essential participants. It was not simply because they had crossed the border to help Subaru and the others, but because they too were victims of 'Gluttony'.

"The important one, Louis, is currently with Rem. Everyone except Anastasia and Julius probably already know about Louis..."

"Of course, dear. When I first learned of it, I was utterly shocked."

"That makes two of us."

Beatrice's assertion that she was surprised, Subaru thought, was likely quite an understatement. In reality, when Subaru first realised that he had been transported alongside Louis, his sole desire to protect Rem led him to adopt a rigid stance. Initially, this caused Rem to regard him with caution, deeming him a man who could not be trusted. Subaru did not know the circumstances under which Emilia and others had encountered Louis, yet it wasn't hard to imagine that their first encounter had been fraught with discord.

Still, he was grateful that no one had resorted to extreme measures.

"You all held up well. Otto and Garfiel, in particular, seemed ready to explode at any moment," he said.

"Indeed, our views really only boiled down to banishment or restraint," replied Otto, "She only managed to avoid those outcomes because Emilia-sama persuaded everyone."

"Emilia did…"

As expected, Otto turned out to be one of the advocates for the radical measures, but it seemed that the more moderate Emilia was able to defuse his fiery disposition.

In response to Subaru's words, Emilia gave a short nod, "Medium-chan tried really hard to protect that girl… Louis. I thought, this child is cherished by so many people around her. That's why it was so frightening to make any decisions right there on the spot."

"I see, Medium…"

Hearing that Medium had protected Louis, Subaru felt a faint sense of relief. Louis had always taken a shine to Medium, who was always bright and very caring. However, when Louis's identity as a Sin Archbishop was revealed, Medium too was among those who feared her.

This was the last contact Subaru had seen of the two back in the Witch's Capital, Chaosflame. But if Medium had come to Louis's defence afterwards, there must have been some change in feelings that Subaru was unaware of. That was a heartening thought.

"I have had a similar experience. A terrifying existence, believed by all to be a witch... I was left all alone, unable to talk to anyone. That's why…"

"In Emilia-sama's case, it was irrational prejudice against half-elves," interrupted Otto. "Her case is different. It's a clear distinction imposed by her own actions."

"Otto..."

When Emilia began to justify her decision to side with Louis in addition to defending Medium, Otto interjected sharply. It was clear he did not hesitate to declare his stance as an ally of Emilia and, at the same time, a foe of Louis.

"Remember, our journey so far has only been on hold until a final decision can be made."

"May I say something?"

The person who raised his hand in ease amidst the tension brought about by Otto's declared stance was Roswaal. As Subaru urged him to speak with his gaze, Roswaal closed his blue eye,

"Myself, Ram, and Lady Anastasia joined the group later. Ultimately, we don't know how the girl named Louis behaves... is there anything suspicious? Surely, Garfiel has been keeping his eyes, ears, and nose open, right?"

"I haven't been sparkling all over the place! B-but, Otto-bro and I agree on this. That's why we kept an eye on her from the moment we ran into her until the big fight..."

"So in the end, you came up empty-handed. Then, on what grounds do you conclude that the girl is a dangerous Sin Archbishop?"

"It's Beatrice and me. We had contact with a girl called Louis Arneb, the "Gluttony," in Priestella. She gouged my foot, so I won't forget it."

Roswaal, as the representative of those who had little connection with Louis, spoke, with Garfiel and Otto expressing their respective views. Otto, who had touched his own foot, was reminded of the injury that had caused his temporary absence from the frontlines - a reasonable explanation why he was particularly wary of Louis.

"I'm sorry to follow up after the Margrave, but may I also speak?"

As Roswaal's question was digested, Julius joined the discussion one after the other. From the beginning, he had a hard look on his face while discussing "Gluttony," and now he glanced at Subaru who seemed even more troubled.

"I would like to confirm, the girl named Louis who is riding in this dragon carriage, there's no mistake that she's Louis Arneb, the Sin Archbishop of Gluttony right?"

"—? What do you mean by that?"

"Both Lady Emilia and Miss Ram should know this. "Gluttony" could change her own appearance to that of the person whose powers she swallowed. In other words..."

"So, this Sin Archbishop of Gluttony and the Louis that Emilia and the others are talking about are not the same person - they might be in a relationship of eater and eaten?"

At Julius's speculation, Emilia and the others made surprised faces, going "ah".

Considering the nature of "Gluttony," it should not be considered an impossible notion. If the archbishop had the ability to reproduce the forms of those it consumed, then the existence of the original victims would make sense, accordingly.

If that were the truth, it would be possible to separate the existence of "Gluttony" from that of Louis.

However...

"...No, that line of thinking is not possible. Louis is likely reproducing the abilities of whoever she's consumed. That must be the result of the authority of "Gluttony"," Subaru discontentedly shakes his head.

"Is that so? I apologize for just causing confusion," Julius closes his eyes and offers an apology.

He is not at fault. If his conjecture was correct, it would have saved Subaru a lot of trouble. However, excuses won't work in this situation.

What is required is not an answer based on shared truths.

With Subaru, Louis was sent to the Vollachian Empire. Despite being treated harshly by Subaru, she struggled many times to aid him, risking her life and getting wounded in the process.

If she is indeed Louis Arneb, the Archbishop of Gluttony, it is necessary to have everyone acknowledge that she is necessary for the upcoming battle.

"...First, allow me to explain in order. Before Emilia-tan met up with Louis, what was it like when I, along with Rem, was carried off to the Empire, living alongside Louis."

"...."

Everyone's gaze focused intensely on him. Despite facing his trusted companions, Subaru felt a certain suffocation. The ordeal he faced was beginning to feel like a combination of grading and cross-referencing.

This was for Louis truly being accepted by the group, for the sake of their verification.

"Subaru, take your time," Emilia softly spoke to the tensed Subaru. Beside him, Beatrice also gave a nod of assent, gently holding Subaru's hand.

Encouraged by their kindness, Subaru took a deep breath and began to speak.

"At the very beginning, when we regained our senses, we were near a large forest far to the east..."

Because Louis did something for Subaru that went beyond what was done to Louis.

"So? Did Barusu do anything rude to you?"

"Splutter!"

Just as she had freed Louis's cheek and put the tea to her mouth, she spat it out in surprise at Ram's words. As she hastily set down her cup, Louis tried to wipe her face with his sleeve.

"It's okay, it's okay, Louis-chan.... Um, what is it, Sister?"

"There is no 'what is it'. Subaru's actions are often rude and insensitive. There is a high chance that he is the reason for Rem's gloomy face, even if it was a random guess."

"That's a bit of an overstatement..."

Upon realizing that her sister's deduction was merely a random guess, Rem, whose face had been wiped by Louis, let out a relieved sigh. However, Ram, who was good at reading people's reactions, seems to have guessed why she had sighed.

Ram sat cross-legged directly in front of her, tilting her cup as she watched Rem intently.

Under that silent pressure, Rem quickly confessed, "About Emilia-san..."

"About Emilia-sama? Yes, Emilia-sama can also be ignorant and impetuous. So that's why Rem's face was darkened."

"N-No, not like that! I just wondered... what's the relationship between Emilia-san and a man named Natsuki Subaru..."

Lowering her voice, Rem succeeded in asking the question as calmly as possible.

However, she had not noticed that her topic of conversation was enough to make Ram squint her light red eyes.

Her intelligent sister murmured a small 'I see,' and said, "Yes, we should tear Subaru apart. Let's do it together later."

"Sister!?"

"Hehe, it's a sisterly collaboration. Even Subaru can be useful sometimes."

Her sister was so beautiful when she smiled faintly, yet her words were incredibly alarming. Hearing Ram's words, Tanza literally got ticked off.

She took a sip of the tea she had made herself, and was surprised at the taste, "Isn't that a little reckless? If you are referring to Lord Schwartz as Subaru, neither I nor the members of the corps will tolerate any harm towards him."

"That's a brave answer. ...Tanza, how old are you?"

"Erm... I'll turn twelve this year."

"Ah, I see. Understood."

"Just because you've reached a conclusion doesn't mean we have."

Rem agreed with Tanza's dissatisfied opinion.

Why did Ram, after hearing Tanza's age, seem to have reached an understanding? In the first place, Ram has not answered Rem's question at all.

What is the relationship between a woman named Emilia and Subaru?

"...They seemed extremely close to each other."

Should someone ask, Rem could only offer a quizzical 'huh?', but there was definitely something strange about the proximity of the two. Regardless, Rem wanted to quickly resolve whatever their situation was so she could focus on her own matters. Her issues, indeed, Rem's problems were—

"They are just that, Rem's pressing concern."

"That's right. It's a trifling matter that I want to settle swiftly so I can address a much bigger problem at hand. It's not more or less than that."

"—Rem, can I have a moment?" "Huh!?"

With a sudden clatter, Rem abruptly stood up in surprise. Her rapid movement accidentally jostled Louis, who was sitting on her lap, causing Rem to lift her into her arms hastily.

As Louis let out a startled "Aou?!", Rem apologized while turning her gaze towards the direction of their knock.

"Who is it?"

"Who else could it be other than Barusu dismembered into eight pieces...? I wonder if the discussion is over."

"Aah..."

Rem let out a small sigh at the words of Ram, who was elegantly savoring her tea. She hugged Louis tighter as he tilted his head questioningly. Burying her nose in his golden hair, Rem closed her eyes.

She had attended the meeting when their convoy was attacked, but did not participate in the subsequent conversation led by a grave-faced Abel with Subaru.

Of course, there was the part where she couldn't offer any significant contribution to the important discussions. And then, there were also the minor doubts she had confirmed to herself a while ago.

But the prime reason was...

"Huh?"

The circumstance with Louis, who was cradled in her arms, was the cause of an unpleasant turmoil stirring in her chest.

Even though she couldn't define it, Rem intuitively felt that she needed to stay by Louis's side. Hence, she retired to her chamber and...

"––– If you please, Sir Schwarz."

"Ah, thank you. ....Wait, so you're here too, Tanza."

"Yes. Everyone, in collusion with you, has dumped me with the housekeeping duties."

"What a guilty way of putting it...."

As Rem was taken aback, Tanza ushered the visitor into the room in her stead.Upon entering, the visitor scratched his head in embarrassment as Tanza lashed out in resentful tones. Despite shrinking in size, the troubled air around his face didn't change in the slightest.

It's probably because his face still retained somewhat of its childlike features, even as he had grown taller.

"Despite his bad eyes, he's a strange man."

"Huh!? Did someone just insult my eyesight? I heard that!"

"Heh. Quite something, isn't it? Complaints from the citizens of the Lugnica Kingdom have traveled beyond the borders."

"There's no way the whole kingdom is talking about my eyes! They must be talking about happier things, like what's for dinner tonight or plans for tomorrow!"

Subaru, responding dramatically to Ram's teasing, stumps into the room.

Somehow, Rem was unable to meet Subaru's gaze and hid her face behind the Louis she was holding, avoiding looking straight at him.

Instead, Louis did the job for her and entertained Subaru with a soft, "Uah-uah."

"Hey, Louis, I had some business with you too. You know where Rem is?"

"Aa-uu."

"I see. You don’t know either. That's a shame, I wanted to have a serious talk."

"――If you want to have a serious talk, stop fooling around and get to the point."

"Wow, Rem! You were hiding behind Louis! I didn’t notice!"

Subaru reacted exaggeratedly and obviously to Rem's interjection. However, Louis seemed to buy into this exaggerated reaction and sadly dropped his shoulders, disappointment visible that he couldn't hide Rem properly.

Putting Louis down on the floor and gently patting her head to comfort her, Rem watched with narrowed eyes.

"......You're not overdoing it, are you?"

"――――"

"I've told you before, please stop overdoing things and taking on reckless challenges. You're not a hero who can do everything."

She echoed the same words she had told him before, seeing Subaru's most recent behavior, it seemed like he was putting on a brave face.

Upon hearing her words, Subaru's eyes widened momentarily, before he let out a wry smile.

"No, actually, objectively speaking I'm Superman. I'm grateful to hear your opinion, Rem, and I'll just take it as your cute way of saying things."

"Please stop kidding around."

"I'm not. ――Rem, there's an important matter about Louis."

Subaru's gaze became serious as he adjusted his posture and erased his wry smile.

As Rem caught her breath at his words, Ram, who had been observing their exchange from the side, sighed,

"Barusu, shall Ram and I leave the room?"

"'Ah, it's nice to have both my sister and Tanza here. My sister serves as the representative of the sisterhood, and Tanza is the representative of the loli department.'

'Ah, so you mean I'm superior to Frederica as a sister. Of course,' a declaration filled with confidence came from Ram.

'Um, what does "loli department"... mean?' Tanza tilted her head in confusion at the request.

Upon the request, Ram puffed out her chest, and Tanza tilted her head.

Then, Rem gently drew in Louis to face Subaru again. In that moment, she held the back of Louis' head into her chest, while attentively examining Subaru.

And then he said...

"Let's settle this. This loving relationship of ours that I don't really get."
