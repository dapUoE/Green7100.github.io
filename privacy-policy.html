Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Greeny AI. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use Greeny AI, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information.

2. Information Collection and Use

Disqus Comments: Our website uses the Disqus platform to enable comments on our content. Disqus may collect information about you when you register or post a comment through its platform. This can include your name, email address, IP address, and other information. We do not have access to this data, and it is managed by Disqus according to their privacy policy, which can be found here.

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze the use of our website. Google Analytics gathers information about website use by means of cookies. The information gathered relating to our website is used to create reports about the use of our website. Google's privacy policy is available at here.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device by websites. They are used to remember preferences, recognize you on subsequent visits, and enhance the user experience.

Our website uses cookies for several purposes:

  • Google Analytics: As mentioned above, to understand how users navigate our website.
  • Disqus: To enable commenting functionality and remember user preferences.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this might affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please review it regularly.

5. Contacting Us

If you have questions about this policy, our data practices, or our dealings with the website, please contact us via email at greenyaicontact@gmail.com.
