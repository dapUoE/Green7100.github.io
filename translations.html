Translations

Here you can find all of the translations I offer. The translations are done using AI, and are not perfect. However I like to prioritise speed and quantity over quality, so I can provide a wide range of translations.

Any translations marked as "Edited" have been proofread by me to iron out any obvious mistakes or incinsistencies by the AI, but does not mean the chapter has been checked by a native speaker. Anything marked as "Unedited" have been freshly generated by the AI, and are not proofread, but are still much more readable than standard MTLs I have come across.

If you would like to request a translation, please visit my contact page, or go to my buy me a coffee commissions page.
